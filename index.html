Hospitality Management System

"Hospitality management System" is to helps employees identify and manage room maintenance needs faster and to check the nearby tourists places from the hotel. It contains two modules User module and Admin module. In admin module admin can add and manages the cities, hotels, tourists places in the cities, and users. In user module user can register and login to the site they can select the cities in that particular city they can view the hotels and tourists places. The users can easily navigate to the selected hotel and book their room by giving the inputs as type of room, no of adults and childeren, period of stay, buffet needed or not. etc and they can book the room and they can easily manage the booking by editing or cancel the booking as well. The users can easily navigate the tourists places nearby the hotel and they can easily navigate the route for the places.

Educational Management System

"Educational Management System" is a project which is developed using Python Full stack i.e. Django. In which in contains 3 Modules they are admin module, Faculty Module and Student Module. Admin Can add students, faculty, classes, courses, subjects etc. and they can provide leave to the faculty or a student as well. Faculty can mark attendance, scores of students, provide leave to the students. Student can view their attendance in particular subjects and marks in that subjects and they seek permission from their portal.
